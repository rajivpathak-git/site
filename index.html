About

I am a molecular biologist with a research focus on understanding therapeutic vulnerabilities in aggressive cancers, particularly triple-negative breast cancer. My work centers on identifying molecular mechanisms that contribute to chemotherapy resistance and exploring strategies to improve treatment outcomes.

I use experimental and analytical approaches in molecular and cellular biology to study cancer signaling pathways and treatment response. Through my research, I aim to contribute to the development of more effective and durable therapeutic strategies with direct relevance to patient care.
